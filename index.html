Japanese Sushi Restaurant & Bar

Welcome to Sushi Spott, a Japanese fusion restaurant with a lounge bar in the heart of Mill Creek, WA. We’re the perfect spot for lunch, dinner, or happy hour—where great food meets your favorite sports on screen. Whether you’re dining in or relaxing at the bar, we’ve got you covered at the Spott.

Not a fan of raw fish? No worries—we specialize in creative custom rolls for every taste. From deep-fried rolls to seared & torched rolls, baked rolls, and house favorites like the Lion King Roll, there’s something for everyone.

Let us take care of you with our extensive menu, featuring fresh nigiri and sashimi, handcrafted specialty rolls, bento boxes, Japanese comfort dishes, teriyaki plates, and udon noodle soup. Looking to unwind? Take a seat at our lounge bar, enjoy a drink, soak in the ambiance, and relax with us at the Spott.
